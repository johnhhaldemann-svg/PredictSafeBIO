## MVP Boundary

PredictSafeBIO is a 30-day MVP/demo foundation, not a validated FDA, GxP, GLP, GMP, GCP, CLIA, CAP, ISO, OSHA, CDC, NIH, EMA, Part 11, or product-release system.

## AI Boundary

- No LLM calls are part of the current engine path.
- The first engine is deterministic and testable.
- AI-assisted document update recommendations must remain draft-only.
- Human review is required before operational, regulatory, quality, clinical, or release decisions.
