# Reformat QA Prompt

Review PredictSafeBIO foundation materials for readability, consistency, and guardrails.

## Checks

- Body text is readable at normal PDF zoom.
- Page breaks do not orphan headings.
- Tables do not overflow or clip text.
- Diagrams use readable labels and are split when dense.
- Naming is consistent:
  - PredictSafeBIO Intelligence Foundation
  - BioType Foundation Packages
  - Core Compliance Components
  - Programs & Methods Library
  - Human Validation Workflow
- Guardrail text appears wherever AI recommendations or drafts are shown.

## Required Guardrail

Draft AI recommendation - human review required. This does not certify compliance, approve documents, close CAPAs, validate systems, or replace qualified review.

## No-Overclaim Check

Reject any artifact that claims FDA approval, GxP validation, regulatory acceptance, Part 11 certification, approved SOP status, closed CAPA status, or guaranteed compliance.
